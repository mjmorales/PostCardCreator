• To Begin, Choose a Picture to upload and click "Add an image to the canvas", New Images will be placed atop older ones.

  • Too add text to the canvas, specify the Font, Font Size, Color, and Decorations then click "Add Text To Canvas", New Text Areas will be placed atop older ones.

  • You may move images and text around the canvas by left clicking and dragging the image.

  • To Resize or Delete images and text, double left click an image or text area to bring up the Scalar Prompt

  • To Save the canvas to a postcard, click "Save Canvas". The Final image will appear below, simply right click and save as image.
